## About Avanzar Rural
- Full name: Proyecto Mejoramiento y Ampliación de los Servicios Públicos para el Desarrollo Productivo Local en los Ámbitos de la Sierra y Selva del Perú
- Funded by FIDA/IFAD: US$71.1M total (US$24M FIDA loan + US$41.1M government)
- Implemented by MINAGRI via Agrorural
- Regions: Amazonas, Ancash, Cajamarca, Lima, San Martín (101 districts)
- Beneficiaries: 20,000+ rural producers, 57,420 direct beneficiaries
- Results: 1,000+ co-financed business plans
- Focus: family farmer productivity, market access, climate resilience, women and youth
- Source: https://gestion.pe/economia/peru-y-fida-firman-acuerdo-de-financiamiento-para-proyecto-avanzar-rural-noticia/
